About Us

Built from the ground up — on real project experience

Swift Support Pte. Ltd. is a Singapore-registered management consultancy specialising in engineering staffing and consultancy for the construction and infrastructure sector.

Our team brings hands-on delivery experience from some of Singapore's most demanding infrastructure programmes — including LTA Mass Rapid Transit contracts, circle and north-south line extensions, underground metro works, and high-rise private developments.

We understand what main contractors and sub-contractors need on the ground — because we have been there. We provide right-fit professionals, fast, without the overhead of large consultancy firms.
